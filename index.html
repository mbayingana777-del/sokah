The Soul of the Caribbean, Smoked in Texas Style

Sokah Caribbean BBQ blends the bold, smoky essence of Texas BBQ with vibrant Caribbean flavors. Each dish transports your taste buds where southern barbecue meets exotic island spices.

Executive Chef Justin Burrus and Pitmaster Cory Brennan expertly smoke meats to perfection, then infuse each dish with Caribbean flair. Every bite tells a story of culinary craftsmanship and passion.

Whether it’s succulent jerk chicken, tender smoked brisket, or savory Oxtail, each meal celebrates flavor, tradition, and innovation.
