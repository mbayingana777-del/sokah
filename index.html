Our Story

The Soul of the Caribbean, Smoked in Texas Style

Sokah Caribbean BBQ was built on more than flavor — it was built on people.

We blend the deep, smoky tradition of Texas barbecue with the bold, vibrant spirit of the Caribbean, creating food that brings everyone to the table. Every dish is crafted with patience, pride, and respect for the cultures that inspire us.

Executive Chef Justin Burrus and Pitmaster Cory Brennan slow-smoke our meats to perfection, then layer in Caribbean spices and techniques passed down through generations. Nothing here is rushed. Every bite tells a story of craft, care, and commitment.

Whether you’re enjoying jerk chicken, tender smoked brisket, or our signature oxtail, you’re not just ordering a meal — you’re joining the Sokah family.
